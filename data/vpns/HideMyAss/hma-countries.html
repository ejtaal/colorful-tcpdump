Some commonly asked questions...

How do I find the fastest server?

Two ways: the easiest, fastest, and generally best way to find the fastest server is to use our Lightning Connect feature, which will automatically find the most optimal server and connect you to it in the blink of an eye. If you’re the kind of person who doesn’t like making a machine do a man’s job, though, you can check each server manually with our built-in Speed Test.

What is a virtual server?

The vast majority of HMA VPN’s servers are physical machines sitting in countries around the world, which you can connect to with a click of a button and browse as if you were there yourself.

A few of them aren’t. These “virtual servers” are physically located outside the country they claim to be in, but use that country’s IP address so it appears as if you’re connecting through that region in question. For example, you could connect to a Cambodian virtual server, and while your network traffic will take you to a physical server in Vietnam, websites will still think you’re in Cambodia. Think of it almost like a VPN within a VPN. VPNception.

Why does HMA VPN use virtual servers?

To keep things running smoothly, generally speaking.

See, to ensure our servers are both high-quality and up to our rigorous security standards, sometimes it’s either very difficult or impossible to have a server physically inside a specific country’s borders, due to that country’s infrastructure, laws, or other complications. So, to make sure you still have options and we don’t have to lower our standards, we use virtual servers, which function exactly as well as normal ones. It's as simple as that.
